🔵 Case Study: 34 Bookings in 2 Days — Without Spending a Dime on Ads

Client Type: Veterinary Clinic (Faroe Islands)
Strategy Used: Organic DM Funnel

🐾 The Challenge

The clinic was entering its slowest season of the year.
No ad budget.
No active outreach on Instagram or Facebook.

Their audience was there… but completely untapped.
They needed a fast, zero-cost way to fill appointments — without overloading their staff or changing internal systems.

🚀 The Strategy

I implemented a plug-and-play Organic DM Funnel that activated their existing audience — fast.

Here’s how we made it work:

  • 🧲 Built a low-barrier, limited-time intro offer tailored to pet owners
  • 📲 Designed story + post templates that sparked interest and urgency
  • 💬 Wrote DM scripts their team could copy-paste to close bookings quickly

All using their current social accounts — with zero additional cost.

📈 The Results

  • 34 total appointments booked in just 48 hours
  • 15 paid consultations → 22,500 DKK in new revenue
  • 0 DKK spent on ads

✅ Fully booked — during one of their slowest months

💡 Why It Worked

This wasn’t a pushy promo or gimmick.
It was a strategic offer backed by urgency, social proof, and friction-free team execution.

We didn’t just drive clicks. We drove cash flow — fast.
