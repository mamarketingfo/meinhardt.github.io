Organic DM Funnel — Fast activation of existing social audience

The Challenge

Heading into a typically quiet season, the clinic was seeing a sharp drop in bookings. They weren’t running ads, and their existing audience on Instagram and Facebook had never been used for direct outreach. They needed a fast way to fill appointments — without investing in ad spend or changing their internal systems.

The Strategy

I implemented a plug-and-play Organic DM Funnel designed to activate their current social media following. The system included:

  • A high-converting intro offer tailored to their ideal clients
  • Engaging story and post templates to spark attention and urgency
  • Done-for-you DM scripts to help their team respond quickly and close bookings fast

All designed to run on their existing accounts — with zero additional cost.

The Results

34
appointments booked within 48 hours
15
paid consultations (22,500 DKK)
0 DKK
ad budget used

Booked out days during one of their slowest months.

Why It Worked

The funnel created a sense of urgency around an irresistible, low-barrier offer. It felt fresh and valuable — not salesy — and gave the clinic's team all the tools they needed to respond quickly, follow up effectively, and close the deal. Fast action. No friction. Real revenue.
