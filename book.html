Our Vetting & Guarantee

We only extend our Growth Guarantee to clinics that meet the following requirements:

  • Consistent monthly revenue of at least $10,000 with the capacity to fulfil increased bookings.
  • Active ad spend or willingness to allocate a minimum of $400 per month to paid acquisition.
  • Dedicated team member ready to implement follow-up and patient experience improvements.

If you qualify, we guarantee a minimum 30% lift in booked appointments within 90 days or we work with you for free until you hit the target.

Who We Partner With

We work best with growth-focused clinic owners who are decisive, collaborative, and positive. If you’re ready to do the work and embrace new ideas, we’ll go all-in for you.

We are not a fit for business owner's who are unmotivated, lazy, and unwilling to put in the work required to be successful. This process takes leadership, optimism, and consistent action.

  • Access: Facebook Page (Admin/Advertiser) or Meta Business Manager invite, Instagram connection, website CMS (viewer), Google Analytics/Tag Manager, booking/CRM (viewer).
